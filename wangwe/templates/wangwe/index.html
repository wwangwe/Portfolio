About

My story begins back when I was a kid. Like other kids, I had a soft spot for superheroes. My icon was Iron Man. I had the dream of one day changing the world like he does. While most kids out-grow the superhero complex,

I could not.

Throughout the (now) two decades of my life, I have lived searching for and identifying matters that I can fix; to make this world a better place. That is how my passion for technology came to grow.

I am curious about a lot of things, how the universe works and why things are the way they are. Most importantly, what needs to be done to get as close as possible to having a perfect world.

I am currently pursuing Bachelor's of Science in Information Technology at Zetech University focusing on Data Science and Machine Learning. I'm also quite experienced in Software Development and Web Technologies.
After graduating, I am hoping to follow my interest in machine leanring to find my way (back) to the realm of engineering.
